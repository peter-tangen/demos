Current Work

The Canvas-Agnostic Rasterizer, or "CAR Engine" is a 3D pixel art renderer for realtime visuals.
It's an immideate mode software renderer written in C, with focus on consistent graphics across
platforms without being dependent on the user's specific graphics hardware, drivers and settings.
