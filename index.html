► About CAR Engine

The renderer responsible for delivering the visual aspects of my current project is called "CAR Engine".
It's a Canvas-Agnostic Rasterizer written in C(99) that, like with the rest of the game tech, runs entirely
in software on the CPU. The main goal of this is portability with consistent visuals that aren't dependent
on the user's specific graphics hardware, drivers and settings. Another goal is to not waste storage space
and network, as it is all quite small! Also, enjoy near-instant loading times on modern computers!
